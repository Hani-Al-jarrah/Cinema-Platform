Cinema Hall Booking

By using our platform, you can easily book your favorite cinema hall for any private event, exclusive movie screening, or social gathering. We are committed to providing a safe, comfortable, and premium cinematic experience tailored to your needs.

Booking & Cancellation Policy

All bookings are confirmed instantly via email once completed through our website. You may cancel your reservation up to 24 hours before the scheduled time for a full refund. Cancellations made within 24 hours of the booking time are non-refundable.

User Responsibilities

Guests are expected to maintain the cinema hall's cleanliness and respect all property. Any damages caused during private bookings will be the responsibility of the booking individual or group. The management reserves the right to refuse service in case of policy violations.

Hall Availability & Restrictions

Cinema hall availability depends on booking schedules and operational hours. Certain films, events, or services might be restricted based on age ratings, content, or management discretion. Always verify hall availability and guidelines before confirming a booking.

  • - Bookings are subject to availability.
  • - Food and beverages policies must be followed.
  • - No unauthorized recording inside cinema halls.
  • - Full payment is required upon booking.
  • - Management has the right to reschedule bookings if necessary.
  • - Guests must adhere to safety regulations.
  • - Refund policies apply as stated.

Privacy & Data Protection

We value your privacy. Personal information collected during the booking process will be securely stored and only used for managing your reservations, promotional updates, and service improvements. We do not share user data with third parties without consent.
